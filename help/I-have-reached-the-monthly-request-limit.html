# I have reached the monthly request limit

Each tier has a monthly request limit. This request limit is the number of requests that you can make to the Apiation API. Once you have reached the monthly request limit, you will not be able to make any more requests to the Apiation API. You will be able to make requests again once the next month starts. Your documentation will still be visible. However, it will not be updated with any changes made after reaching the limit. This means that the data in your documentation could risk becoming outdated. You are always free to upgrade your plan to a higher tier, which will increase your monthly request limit. If you are currently on the professional plan, and you have reached the monthly request limit, you can contact us at contact@apation.io
