# When is my documentation visible?

When you start recording your API, we will start building your documentation website. This process can take up to 24 hours. Once the process is complete, you will be able to access your documentation website.

# At what interval does my documentation update?

We use a 7 day rolling window to update your documentation. This means that it can take up to 7 days for your documentation to update. We do this to ensure that we keep your documentation up to date, while reducing the risk of showing incorrect data.

# How to force an update

Currently this option is not available. We are working on a way to force an update. contact@apation.io
